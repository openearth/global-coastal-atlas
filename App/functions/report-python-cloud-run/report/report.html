Beach Sediment Classification

The Shoreline Monitor

The coast in this area is characterized by a mix of accretion and erosion. Significant portions exhibit stable conditions, with change rates between -0.5 and 0.5 m/yr. However, there are areas of intense accretion (1 to 3 m/yr) and severe accretion (3 to 5 m/yr), particularly around 4.19°E and 51.43°N. Extreme accretion (>5 m/yr) is also observed in some segments. Conversely, intense erosion (-3 to -1 m/yr) and severe erosion (-5 to -3 m/yr) are noted, especially near 4.18°E and 51.51°N. Extreme erosion (<-5 m/yr) is present in isolated locations, indicating significant coastal dynamics.
